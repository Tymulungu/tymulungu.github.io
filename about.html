About me

Aspiring Web Developer

My name is Tyler Moore and I am an aspiring web developer. After returning from a religious mission is South Africa, I felt a strong urge to follow my dream of becoming a programmer. I had originally though that I wanted to study C++ and become and very deep down and dirty programmer for windows systems. After buying myself a Macbook and learning html on Codecademy I decided that web development was my passion. I love seeing my work manifested in the visual aspects. I am currently attending school in Logan, Utah and loving it! I am always creating projects for myself and my friends and family. I am excited to delve into this world of developing.
